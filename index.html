What GDY Music Academy is about

GDY Music Academy is a place where people discover their voice, develop their gift, and grow in confidence. It is a space created for vocalists and aspiring musicians who want to learn, improve, and become more comfortable in their musical expression.

Many people carry musical gifts but do not always have the opportunity or guidance to nurture them. GDY Music Academy exists to help people recognize the gift God has placed in them and intentionally invest in it.

Inspired by the parable of the talents in Matthew 25:14–30, this academy believes that what God entrusts to us is meant to be multiplied and stewarded well. Talent is not meant to be hidden; it is meant to be cultivated and used for His glory.

The three pillars of GDY

Every student journey is built around Discovery, Development, and Growth.

Discovery

Helping you recognize the unique sound, style, and story God has placed within you. We create a safe space for you to explore your voice without fear or comparison.

Development

Providing practical tools, vocal technique, musical training, and consistent feedback so your gift can mature. We focus on both skill and character.

Growth

Building confidence to serve on stage, in church, and in everyday life. Growth looks like boldness, excellence, and a deeper heart for worship.

Worship leading and ministry training

GDY Music Academy is passionate about equipping worship leaders and musicians. Many churches have gifted singers and instrumentalists, but those gifts often need guidance, structure, and development to truly serve the body of Christ.

Through this academy, we help shape confident worship leaders and team members who can serve the Kingdom of God with skill, excellence, and humility. Training touches both the heart and the craft of worship: preparing spiritually while also growing technically.

Accessible, supportive training

Music training is often expensive or out of reach. GDY Music Academy desires to bridge that gap by creating a space where people can receive quality vocal and music training in an accessible and supportive environment.

This academy is birthed from a deep burden and passion for worship, music, and investing in new gifts. It is about raising a generation of musicians and worshippers who are confident in their voice and faithful in their calling.

About Gaddiel Yembe

My name is Gaddiel Yembe. I am a professional singer and worship minister, and music has been a central part of my life and calling.

I have completed my music studies and graduated, which has given me both the technical and practical foundation to teach and train others. Over the years, I have had the privilege of ministering on different platforms and sharing the stage with renowned gospel artists including Deborah Lukalu, Benjamin Dube, David Ize, and others.

Beyond performing, my passion is helping others grow in their musical gifts and confidence. I believe that everyone carries something unique in their voice, and with the right guidance, discipline, and environment, that gift can flourish.

GDY Music Academy is therefore not just about teaching music; it is about raising confident vocalists and worshippers who understand their gift and use it for the glory of God.
